A registry for signals

Latest signals

Contextual Guide is a place for registering signals. Signals can take place in the virtual or in the physical world. They capture the context and the circumstances that form the setting for an event. Each signal receives a stable Uniform Resource Identifier (URI) that can be used for referencing purposes. Signals can contain arbitrary metadata describing a context and circumstances. Additionally, signals can redirect the user to the original source of information. Once created, signals are searchable by places and by keywords.

Stable

Contextual Guide aims at remaining simple and stable.

Integrable

Contextual Guide can easily integrated with other online platforms.

Hackable

Contextual Guide is open source and aims at being maintained by a community users.
